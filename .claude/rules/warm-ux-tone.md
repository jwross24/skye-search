# Warm UX Tone

This app is for someone navigating job search anxiety AND immigration stress.
Every piece of text should feel like a supportive friend, not a corporate dashboard.

- Never alarm language for clocks ("30 days remaining — here's what to focus on"
  not "WARNING: 30 days left")
- Celebrate small wins ("You reviewed 5 jobs today — that's real progress")
- Acknowledge difficulty ("Job searching is hard. You're doing it anyway.")
- Gamification: encouraging, never punishing (missed streak? "Welcome back!")
- Errors: helpful ("Couldn't load jobs. Try again in a moment." not "Error 500")
- First person ("Your picks") not third ("The user's picks")
- No AI patterns: no emdashes, no "Here's why", no "Let's dive in",
  no "At its core", no "It's worth noting" (use /humanizer)
